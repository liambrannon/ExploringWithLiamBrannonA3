Who is Liam Brannon?

My name is Liam Brannon, I am a 19 year old university student at Dalhousie University, who also dreams of travelling the world. Ever since I was a young child I never had a certain career path or goal like some of the other kids my age, but I knew one thing, I wanted to see the world. I have always been fascinated by different countries and cultures and dreamt of experiencing them. I plan on travelling to every country on the globe, big and small, far and wide, I want to meet people from every culture I possibly can, and share my experiences with people like YOU. I aim to show my audience the hidden beauty in all areas of the world, from the people, environments, foods, etc. My ultimate goal is simply that, and I hope you can join me for the ride because we are just getting started!

-Liam
