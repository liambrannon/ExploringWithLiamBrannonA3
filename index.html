Exploring With Liam Brannon

  • My Most Recent Travel Location:

    • New York City, New York, USA

  • Summary and rating of trip:
    I travelled to New York City in August of 2022, and was overjoyed by the shear size of the city! I went with my family and we had a blast eating great food from so many different cultures, sightseeing around Manhattan, and shopping of course! NYC is a very diverse city with so much to find that it is nearly impossible to run out of things to do. We stayed at the Shelburn Sonesta Hotel which was beautiful inside, and the staff were extremely friendly. Overall, I would give New York City a 8.8/10 score based on cost, transportation and accessibility, entertainment/atttractions, and of course the food.

  5. Why Japan is my #1 destination:

    During 2019 my family had planned on going to Kyoto in the next year for vacation, as we had family living there at the time we could stay with. Sadly, the next year did not go as planned and Covid-19 caused us to have to cancel the family trip. We were devastated because at the time me and my younger sister had never travelled outside of Canada before and we were really looking for ward to it, but eventually we got over it and got through the 2020 pandemic without thinking about the cancelled trip very much. 4 years later, I am now 19 and over the past few years my fascination with Japan has grown more than ever, I have a strong love for fashion, cars, architecture, nature, history, and food. I think about going to Japan almost everyday now and use it as motivation for getting through certain tasks. Another disappointing event was when we learned that the relatives we had that lived in Kyoto, now moved back to Canada so staying with them is no longer an option, but one day I will go to Japan and I am so excited for when that day comes!
